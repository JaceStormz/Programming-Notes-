Company Mission

  1. Be able to select drinks for from a list of premade drinks or build their own drinks/meals and submit their custom-made drinks/meals for others to try out on their own.
  2. All ingredients are organically grown vegetables and fruits with additions like nuts oats seeds. These drinks cater to the health conscious to the diet restricted such diabetics and food allergic.
  3. The focus toward this is a need to stay healthy by health-conscious decisions of the food that we ingest into ones body.

Goals

  1. This website shall focus on the way the ingredients are grown as well as how the ingredients are combined to create healthy and memorable drinks that can be consumed by the general public.
  2. The website will also provide informational knowledge that will bring a curious insight to the drinks and meals that are being consumed. All prices are based on the regional pricing of the ingredients.
  3. There will also include build your own beverage/meals to satisfy all types of drink and meal combinations.
